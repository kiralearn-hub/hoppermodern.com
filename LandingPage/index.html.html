Our approach

The Organizational Gap Analysis.

A four-step, evidence-led diagnostic that surfaces the real reason behind the loss — not the convenient one. Designed by a Top 10 CLO, refined across Fortune 500s and high-growth startups.

Output

A 30-page Gap Diagnostic + a single-page CEO thesis.

  1. 01

    Listen, structurally

    Quant + qual. Pulse, NPS, attrition cohorts, BU P&L, manager 1:1s, exit interviews — read together, not in silos.

  2. 02

    Talk to the humans

    Founder, CHRO, frontline, customers who left. We surface what's truly stuck — not what's politically convenient.

  3. 03

    Map the gap

    We connect culture, capability, structure, leadership and L&D into one diagnostic. One picture. One thesis.

  4. 04

    Quantify the loss

    Translate the gap into ₹/$ — revenue at risk, productivity drag, replacement cost, customer LTV erosion.
